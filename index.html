KIRKLAND COMMUNITY

General information

Located in Washington, Kirkland is a suburb east of Seattle. Its population was around 92,000 from the 2020 US census. Kirkland is known for its Lake Washinton waterfront, which consists of outstanding views, a diverse crowd of restaurants, and many art galleries. The company Costco previously had headquarters in the city of Kirkland, and Kirkland is the namesake of that company.

Key characteristics

Kirkland is a city located on the eastern shore of Lake Washington. it is known for its nice downtown area, scenic parks, and a strong sense of community. The city blends a small-town energy but is also near many big cities, making it lively and comfortable.

Notable Features

Kirkland features beautiful waterfront areas, public beaches, marinas, and well maintained parks like Marina Park or Juanita Bay Park. It also has a thriving arts and culture scene, local shops, resturaunts, and frequent community events that bring the people in kirkland together.

More About Kirkland

Kirkland offers a high quality way of life with good schools, safe neighborhoods, and lots of outdoor activites. There is lots of nature surrounding kirkland, and strong community involvment. Kirkland is in the proximity of many major job centers making it a well-balanced place to live for people of any age.
